The shape of the year

A streak is output. A platform is an asset.

The streak

  • Daily posting keeps it alive
  • Effort measured in days survived
  • Ends on day 365 with a habit
versus

The platform

  • A recognizable identity
  • Pillars people return for
  • A path from viewer to client

Your 365 days are the raw material. The system we’re building is what turns the material into the platform — that’s the difference between ending the year with a habit and ending it with an asset.

Deliver this slowly. It governs the year. This is the frame he should repeat back in his own words before the call ends. If he describes the year as building a platform rather than keeping a streak, the frame landed. Transition: “Good. Let’s close this out properly.”
